## ğŸš€ How to Use

### For Parents and Educators

1. **Choose the appropriate level** for the child's age and ability
2. **Don't do everything at once** - we recommend 2-3 exercises per session
3. **Use concrete materials** - real coins, drawings, physical piggy bank
4. **Adapt the values** - use real prices of objects the child desires
5. **Celebrate achievements** - when goals are reached, celebrate!
